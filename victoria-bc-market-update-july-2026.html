What buyers should do now

  • Set alerts by exact neighbourhood and property type, not broad city-wide filters only.
  • Get financing and documentation ready before touring so you can act quickly on strong listings.
  • Track listing tenure and comparable activity to calibrate offer structure and timing.

What sellers should do now

  • Price from current comparables and active competition, not older peak-period anchors.
  • Use launch-week marketing strength: presentation quality and clarity of positioning still matter.
  • Prepare for negotiation depth; strongest outcomes come from plan + execution, not list price alone.
